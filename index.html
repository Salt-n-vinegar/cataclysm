Cataclysm Explained

Forget Ancient Mayan predictions. Have you noticed how silky cats are looking lately? Something isn't right. After an arduous day of trekking through some of the most rugged local parks known to man, as well as petting many cute cats along the way, I came to the conclusion that another bad Hollywood movie is unfolding. It's predicted - by me - that cats will become the owners of the planet, dethroning humans and relegating them to being "Homo Lamus." Naturally, this will be known as "Cataclysm."

Maybe. Have you ever been around a cat and noticed it observing you for a really long time? Don't they know it's rude to stare? Yes, they do, but your feelings are #71 on their priorty list. The moment you lose the staring contest, you've established who's wearing the pants. Also, have you ever noticed how super affectionate cats are, only for them to leave soon after? It's almost like they're getting close enough to gather information and then abruptly leaving to file a sitrep.

Did you ever read Sun Tzu's 'The Art of War'? Me neither. That leaves you with two choices below to help you prepare for Cataclysm. Check out some images of cats being cats, or learn a super fun fact about them. When they take over, maybe you can impress them with a fact and get preferential treatment. That'll make you a traitor to the humans, but their opinions won't matter after Cataclysm anyway, right? Good luck. If the APIs aren't working below, you're on your own.
